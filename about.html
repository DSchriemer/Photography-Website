Thanks for visiting my website. I've always had a passion for photography and technology, so it was only a matter of time before I built this website to share some of my favourite captures. I bought my first camera 2 years ago, and the process of learning how to take and edit photos has been a more rewarding challenge than I ever expected.

Some of you will know that I dislike sharing my photos on social media. It felt wrong to share some of my most personal experiences with people I dont talk to, and on platforms as busy and unorganized as Instagram and Facebook. I wanted a way to share my photos with the people close to me, but there was nothing out there that fit what I was looking for. Ultimately I decided that building my own website was the best way to do this, as I could keep it between close friends and family while being able to work with it in any way I wanted.

Thanks for visiting my site, I'm looking forward to our adventures in the future.

-David
