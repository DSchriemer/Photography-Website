Thanks for visiting my website. I've always had a passion for photography and technology, so it was only a matter of time before I built this site to share some of my favourite experiences.

I've always loved taking photos, but when I bought my first DSLR 2 years ago I realized that there was so much potential behind the photos I was taking. I became determined to improve how I took and edited photos. I wanted to go beyond standard nature photography by working with things like light and color to describe experiences or stories. Through this my goal is to make art out of photos.

Thanks again for checking this site out! I dont use social media for sharing my photos so If you like what you see keep following this site as I update it often. I'm looking forward to sharing more adventures in the future.

-David
